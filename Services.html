Our Services

Plastic Recycling

Our plastic recycling plants recycle plastic waste into usable products such as waste bins, polyethylene bags commonly known as plastic bags and waste bin liners. Our Waste Bins comes in the form of 240 litres and 120litres suitable to store your waste.

E-Waste Recycling

Collecting and recycling electronic waste, such as mobile phones, and other electronic devices. Secure data destruction and recycling of IT equipment.Components that are still in good working condition, such as hard drives can be reused in other devices or resold as refurbished parts.

Hazardous Waste Management

Managing the proper disposal and recycling of hazardous materials, such as batteries and more.Hazardous waste management protect people and the environment through safety measures during material handling and transport.Ensuring compliance with environmental rules.

Material Sorting and Processing

Material Recovery Facilities (MRFs). Sorting, separating, and preparing recyclables for resale to manufacturers. Shredding and Compacting. Reducing the volume of materials like paper and cardboard for more efficient transport.

Consulting and Education.

we offer consulting and education services to empower individuals, businesses, and organizations with the knowledge and strategies needed to make informed, eco-conscious choices. Our goal is to promote recycling, waste reduction, and sustainability.

Waste Collection Services

Through waste collection services, we ensure the efficient gathering of recyclable materials, which are then processed to create a range of valuable end products. These include recycled paper for new paper products, recycled plastics used in various plastic items, and recycled metals that are melted and reused in manufacturing

Textile Recycling

Textile recycling efficiently extracts and processes fibers from old clothing and fabrics, removing impurities to create high-quality materials. These reclaimed fibers are used to make new textiles and garments, reducing waste and lowering the fashion industry's environmental impact.

Metal Recycling

Metal recycling processes recover and recycle a variety of metals, such as aluminum, copper, and steel. These reclaimed metals are essential for manufacturing new, sustainable metal products, reducing the environmental impact and conserving valuable resources.
